Foundation

Color System

All warm brown — no pure black, no cool grey anywhere. Espresso is the deepest dark. Antique Gold and Aged Brass are the two accent weights.

Foundation

Typography

Two typefaces only. Cormorant Garamond for display, headings, and quotes (500 weight italic for pull quotes). Fraunces for all body text, labels, and UI.
